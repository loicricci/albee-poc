## Color Usage Philosophy

### Primary (`#2E3A59` - Deep Slate Blue)
- Main accent color throughout
- Used for: Buttons, icons, secondary text, hover states
- Creates professional, trustworthy feel
- Provides excellent contrast against white

### Secondary (`#C8A24A` - Muted Amber)
- Used sparingly as specified
- Highlights: Badge pulse, viewers section, intimate tier
- Adds warmth and premium feel
- Creates visual interest without overwhelming

### Neutral (`#E6E6E6` - Grey)
- Borders and subtle backgrounds
- Creates soft separation
- Maintains clean, minimal aesthetic
- Never harsh or distracting

### Base (`#0B0B0C` - Black)
- Primary text and headings
- Strong, readable contrast
- Professional and sophisticated
- Slightly warmer than pure black

---

## Design Principles Applied

1. **Hierarchy through color**
   - Black for primary content
   - Deep Slate Blue for secondary/accent
   - Grey for structure
   - Amber for special highlights

2. **Opacity for subtlety**
   - Text at 70-80% for body copy
   - Backgrounds at 10-30% for soft accents
   - Borders at 30% for gentle emphasis

3. **Consistency**
   - Same colors used across similar elements
   - Predictable hover states
   - Coherent visual language

4. **Restraint with amber**
   - Only 3 uses: Badge pulse, viewers section, intimate tier
   - Creates special moments
   - Doesn't compete with primary accent
